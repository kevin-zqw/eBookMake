To Robert Landry

1950.8.26

Dear Mr.Landry,

I accept The Authors Guild invitation to join The Authors League of America, Inc. I understand that my membership dues will be $15 a year. Notices and announcements should be sent to me at: The Artists'Association Of Peking, 15, Hsia Rung Fu, East City, Peking, China.

Yours Very Truly,

Lan Shaw
