（Telex）

Mr.Chao Cha-pi

Zung Kwlng Publishing Company

Two Fifteen Szechan Road

（Central）

Shanghai, China

July 26, 1948

Important to establisth validity of copyright on Divorce. Please cable reply prepaid to me care my American publishers address Harbrace New York following information after check with ministry of interior where necessary and possible: were all formalities requisite Chinese copyright nineteen thirtythree edition complied with, if so please state various steps taken to secure copyright, also whether title page of nineteen thirtythree edition contained notice of copyright and if so exact wording and whether in English or Chinese. Also please state what similar steps taken in connection with nineteen fortyseven edition and any other edition issued. Plesse supply any other pertinent information you may have.

S.Y.Shu
